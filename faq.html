Frequently Asked Questions

  • Production time for an order only begins once a proof of payment has been received.
  • Production time is 2 - 4 business days. (EXCLUDING weekends)
    • Delays may take place due to unforeseen circumstances. If this is the case, the customer will be informed (eg. Power cuts, medical emergencies and so forth).
  • Rush orders will be accepted at a 25% price increase.
  • Delivery time is 2 - 5 business days. (EXCLUDING weekends)
  • Each order is made specifically for you, and your requests. We do not keep ready-made stock.
  • During holidays, special occasions (Valentine's Day, Easter, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Christmas) and sales, production time may be slightly longer.

  • We handle the delivery of our products through PUDO for a cheaper delivery cost for you!
    • PUDO to locker: You pick the locker that you want your package shipped to.
    • PUDO to door: Your package gets delivered straight to your door, at an extra cost.
      • Please note we cannot be held responsible for you being at home when they deliver.

  • Cancellations can only be made within 24 hours after you’ve placed your order (an order is defined as the moment the payment has been made). If you’re within that time frame, email us at wholeheartsa@gmail.com.
  • A 30% cancellation fee is applicable.

  • Due to the nature of the products sold by My Whole Heart, we are unable to accept returns or exchanges to our products.
  • Personalized products are made using the spelling provided by the customer. If the names sent in for the order were spelt incorrectly, fixing a mistake after it has already been printed will count as another product, and will be priced as such.
  • If a production mistake was made on our side, please contact wholeheartsa@gmail.com and we will resolve the issue asap!

  • Please ensure that names are submitted with the correct spelling.
  • Please ensure that you provide the correct address.
  • PO Boxes are not accepted.
  • My Whole Heart cannot take responsibility or ensure that anyone is home to accept your parcel.
  • Delivery only takes place during business hours from Monday-Friday.
  • We cannot ensure exact delivery dates or times, as shipping is done with an independent company.

Thank you for your support, from the bottom of My Whole Heart.
